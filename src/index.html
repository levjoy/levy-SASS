Hello

I’m Josh Levy. I’m a technologist, digital rights advocate, musician, dad, New Englander, and dog owner.

I’m CTO and co-founder of Center for Digital Resilience (CDR), a global NGO that facilitates digital security support for communities around the world. I was previously Advocacy Director at Access Now, Campaign Director at Free Press, among other things. I was a 2017 non-resident fellow at Stanford’s Digital Civil Society Lab and I hold an MFA in Integrated Media Arts from Hunter College, City University of New York.

I write and play music. My bands are Outro and All Feels. Outro’s first full-length The Current was released in the summer of 2023.

You can find me on my personal Mastodon instance. I also co-admin mastodon.ngo, a server for non-profits, NGOs, and people working in civil society and human rights.
