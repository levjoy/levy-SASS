Outro

“REM on a space-rock trip… almost an American version of Echo and the Bunnymen.” - Ether Diver

Outro is a pysch-pop post-punk band out of western MA. Our DNA is spliced from local bands including Psychic Energy, the Capitulators, Mobius Band, the Lucky Shots, and Bring it to Bear. We mix elements of the melodic psych-rock and off-kilter punk of bands like Deerhunter, Bedhead, Built to Spill, and the Feelies.

We released our first full-length The Current in July 2023. You can find us on Bandcamp, Spotify, Apple Music, and YouTube.

Send me an email at josh AT levjoy DOT com to inquire about shows or just to say hi!
